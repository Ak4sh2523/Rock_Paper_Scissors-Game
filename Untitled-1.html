Rock Paper Scissors

Objective:
The objective of the game is to defeat your opponent by selecting a weapon that defeats their choice.

Weapon Choices:
There are three possible choices: Rock, Paper, and Scissors. Rock defeats Scissors (Rock crushes Scissors) Scissors defeats Paper (Scissors cut Paper) Paper defeats Rock (Paper covers Rock).

Gameplay:
Players select one of the three weapons simultaneously and reveal their choices at the same time.

Outcome:
The outcome is determined based on the interaction between the chosen weapons: If both players choose the same weapon, the game is a tie. Otherwise, the player who chose the winning weapon (based on the rules above) wins the round.
